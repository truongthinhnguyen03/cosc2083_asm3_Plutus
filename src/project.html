Topic Overview

Our group topic is a financial management web application, supported for personal spending and the team’s budget. For individuals, this application will allow users to keep track of their daily expenses, and payment history through visualization and comparison of spending among different periods. Based on the result, users are given recommendations to adjust their expenditure on specific categories and notified when the spending level exceeds the proposed budget. On a larger scale, regarding teams, the application would enable team managers to monitor organizational expenses, ensuring that they are within budget. In addition, team members’ budgets are distributed by the manager and their spending will be recorded for better tracking.
